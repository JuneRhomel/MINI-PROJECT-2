the OnlineEdu

Our goal is to create the best possible elementary school without having to pay for the learning services we offer. If we succeed with this, it will give kids a very creative way of learning. Regardless of where they live and the size of their wallets.
To achieve this, we have created multiple modules, excercises and visual representation of the most popular subjects in Elementary education. This will provide a remote learning system for kids that could help them learn a lot while having fun.
If you're an educator who's interested in creating a module,excercises or visual presentations on OnlineEd, please send us an email with an example video where you teach a topic to teachers@online.edu.
